About Me

Hi, my name is Austin (aka Dickey). I've been playing video games for 15+ years now. I just recently became a Twitch streamer back in June of 2020. I love to bring the people content, but more importantly, I just want my viewers to have a good time. I'm not a top notch player, but strive to be a better gamer each time I touch the sticks. I play on the original Xbox One, and I use an AverMedia capture card to add my overlays to the stream.

I'm currently on the grind to reach affiliate status from Twitch, and I could use all the support y'all are able to offer. A simple follow actual means more to me than you could ever know. Just dropping by the stream for a few minutes and hitting me with a couple of messages in the chat brightens my evenings. I'm in the process of trying to build a community, and I would love to have you in it. Life is short, so lets enjoy each day together!
